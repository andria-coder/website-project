About

Taiwanese-American violinist Andria Chang studied with Prof. Ariadne Daskalakis and holds Bachelor and Master of Music degrees in Violin and Chamber Music from Hochschule für Musik und Tanz Köln and Hochschule für Musik Hanns Eisler Berlin in Germany.

As an enthusiastic chamber musician, Andria is a member of Synergia Piano Trio. She has collaborated with others at music festivals such as the International Chamber Music Week in Montepulciano (IT), Beethovenfest Bonn, Acht Brücken Festival, Moers Festival, International Sommerakademie Frenswegen (DE), Music from Land’s End Wareham and the International Musical Arts Institute Fryeburg (USA).

Since 2018, she has been the assistant to the Artistic Director of Palazzo Ricci European Academy for Music and Performing Arts in Montepulciano, Italy and has been a faculty member of HfMT Köln since 2021.

Andria played in the Mahler Chamber Orchestra as an academist and has worked with amongst others Beethoven Orchester Bonn, Gürzenich Orchester, Kölner Kammerorchester, Wuppertal Sinfonieorchester.

Focusing in historical performance practice, she has performed and recorded with early music ensembles such as Capella Augustina, Cölner Barockorchester, Compagnia di Punto, Concerto Köln, Deutsche Händel-Solisten, Ensemble Vintage Köln, Harmonie Universelle, Kölner Akademie and Orchester l’arte del mondo.

Andria currently resides in Cologne, Germany.
